Koman Lake Day Tour

Activity


Our tour begins in the morning going through the beautiful villages with a van, It takes 2 hours to go to koman lake during this time we will take a break for coffee at the harbor. Then we will take the boat tour which is considered to be ranked as one of the best boat tour in the world. During this time we will be sailing across the alpine mountains. You will have the opportunity to see few houses in the mountains which are accessable only by boat. Also we will stop for swimming, enjoying the crystal water of koman lake As we explore the nature we will also go to the families who live there and enjoy the delicious food this place offers And if you are not a tourist but an investor you can be a motivator to amplify the production of village bio foods and drinks.
